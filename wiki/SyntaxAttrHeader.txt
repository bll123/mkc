### header (attribute)

The __header__ attribute specifies the required headers for a
[check](SyntaxCheck). The headers specified will be added to the
[check](SyntaxCheck).

    header <header-name> [<header-name> ...];

The __header__ attribute is used with a [check](SyntaxCheck)
statement or with an [alternate](SyntaxAttrAlternate) attribute.

Multiple __header__ attribute statements may be included.

Repeatable: __yes__

##### Examples

    header sys/statvfs.h;
    header stdint.h stdbool.h;
