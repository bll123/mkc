### Syntax: compiler (attribute)

    compiler <compiler-name>;

The _compiler_ attribute is used for [project](SyntaxProject)
and [profile](SyntaxProfile) statements.

In a [project](SyntaxProject) statement, the default compiler will be
set to _compiler-name_.

In a [profile](SyntaxProfile) statement, the compiler is set
for the following statements in the profile statement block.

As of 2026-6-18, the compiler names that are recognized are 'c',
'c++', 'objc', 'bison' and 'flex'.

Repeatable: no
