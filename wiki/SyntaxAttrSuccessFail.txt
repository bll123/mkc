### success, failure (attribute)

The __success__ or __failure__ attribute specifies a block of
statements to run on success or failure of a [check](SyntaxCheck). It
is used in all [check](SyntaxCheck) statements.

    success { <statement-list> }
    failure { <statement-list> }

Repeatable: no
