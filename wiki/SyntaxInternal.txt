### Syntax: Internal Variables

##### Compiler Identification (boolean)

    MKC_COMPILER_ID_CLANG
    MKC_COMPILER_ID_GCC
    MKC_COMPILER_ID_ICC
    MKC_COMPILER_ID_MSC
    MKC_COMPILER_ID_SOLARIS
    MKC_COMPILER_ID_XLC
    MKC_COMPILER_ID_UNKNOWN       # not determined

##### System Identification (boolean)

    MKC_SYS_AIX
    MKC_SYS_ANDROID
    MKC_SYS_BSD
    MKC_SYS_LINUX
    MKC_SYS_MACOS
    MKC_SYS_SOLARIS
    MKC_SYS_WINDOWS
    MKC_SYS_UNKNOWN               # not determined

##### System Specific Identification (boolean)

    MKC_SYS_ID_CYGWIN
    MKC_SYS_ID_DRAGONFLYBSD
    MKC_SYS_ID_FREEBSD
    MKC_SYS_ID_MSYS2
    MKC_SYS_ID_NETBSD
    MKC_SYS_ID_OPENBSD
    MKC_SYS_ID_NOTSET             # not determined

##### System Information

    MKC_EXECUTABLE_EXTENSION      (string)
    MKC_LIB_LOC_LIB64             (Linux only, boolean)  # subject to change
    MKC_OBJECT_EXTENSION          (string)
    MKC_SHARED_LIBRARY_EXTENSION  (string)

##### Compilers (string, from environment if present)

    BISON bison
    CC cc
    CXX c++
    FLEX flex
    OBJC objc
