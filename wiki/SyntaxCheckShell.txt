### Check Shell Extract

Sets variables from a shell script.  Only variable assignments are
processed.  Substitution is done, but environment variables are not
recognized.

2026-6-28 Only recognizes ${var}, not $var.  Environment variables are
not recognized.

    check_shell_extract <shell-script>;

##### Examples

    check_shell_extract VERSION;

VERSION:

    VERSION=1.2.3
    BUILD_NUMBER=1249
    DIR=mydir/${VERSION}

##### Attributes

No attributes may be specified.
