### Syntax: compiler (attribute)

    compiler <compiler-name>;

The _compiler_ statement is an attribute for a [project](SyntaxProject)
statement.

The default compiler will be set to _compiler-name_.

As of 2026-6-15, the compiler names that are recognized are 'c', 'c++'
and 'objc'.
