### Check Link Flag

    check_link_flag '-fsanitize=address';

Checks to see if a link flag is valid, and if so, adds it to
LDFLAGS for the current profile.

Generated Name: Prefix: __lf\___
<br>Any leading '-' or '-W' is removed from the name.
<br>Example: \_lf\_fsanitize_address

##### Adding a Link Flag

    add-link-flag '-DNO_DEPRECATED';

Adds a link flag without any check.

##### Examples

    check_link_flag '-fPIC';
    check_link_flag '-lintl';
