### Syntax: Directives

##### Internal Debugging

    mkcdebug printvar <profile>;    # user defined profile
    mkcdebug printvar release;
    mkcdebug printvar global;
    mkcdebug printvar internal;
    mkcdebug printvar internalall;  # including variables used internally
    mkcdebug printvar test;         # most global variables
    mkcdebug printprof;             # prints profile names
