### Syntax: Directives

##### Internal Debugging

    mkcdebug printinternal;         # prints internal values
    mkcdebug printprof;             # prints profile names
    mkcdebug printvar <profile>;    # user defined profile
    mkcdebug printvar internal;
    mkcdebug printvar internalall;  # including temporary variables
    mkcdebug printvar test;         # most variables
