### Check Header

Checks if _header_ exists as a macro.

    check_define <header>;
    check_define <header> { <attribute-statements> }

Generated Name: Prefix: __\_header\___
<br>Example: \_header\_sys\_stat

##### Examples

    check_header sys/stat.h;

* [header](SyntaxAttrHeader)
* [name](SyntaxAttrName)
* [compiler_flags](SyntaxAttrCompileFlag)
