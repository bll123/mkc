### Syntax: name (attribute)

    name <new-name>;

The __name__ attribute is an attribute for [check](SyntaxCheck)
statements and for [project](SyntaxProject) statements.

For [check](SyntaxCheck) statements, the __name__ attribute replaces
the generated name of the check with _new-name_.

For [project](SyntaxProject) statements, the __name__ attribute
defines the name of the project.
