### Syntax: Name (attribute)

    name <new-name>;

The name statement is an attribute for [check](SyntaxCheck)
statements and for [project](SyntaxProject) statements.

For [check](SyntaxCheck) statements, the _name_ statement replaces the
generated name of the check with _new-name_.

For [project](SyntaxProject) statements, the _name_ statement sets the
name of the project.
