### Check Struct Member

Checks to see if _member-name_ exists in _struct-name_.

    check_struct_member <struct-name> <member-name>;
    check_struct_member <struct-name> <member-name> { <attribute-statements> }

Generated Name: Prefix: __\_member\___
<br>Example: \_member\_struct\_stat\_st\_mode

##### Examples

    check_struct_member 'struct stat' st_mode {
      header sys/stat.h;
    }
