### path (attribute)

The __path__ attribute specifies an additional path.  It is used in
the [check_package](SyntaxCheckPackage) statement and the
[check_include_dependencies](SyntaxStmtCheckIncDeps) statement.

    path <path>;

Repeatable: __yes__
