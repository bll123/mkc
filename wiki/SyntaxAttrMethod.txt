### Syntax: method (attribute)

The __method__ attribute is used to specify the processing method. It is
used in the [configure](SyntaxConfigure) statement.

    method <method>;

For a [configure](SyntaxConfigure) statement, _method_ may be one of
_auto_ or _manual_.

Repeatable: no
